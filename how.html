The best way to have a car. Share if you want to.

Rent out your car and cover your costs

You can choose to rent out your GoMore leasing car when it’s not being used. Use our little calculator to figure out what your next car could cost a month.

We can also pre-install your car with Keyless technology. That means renters can unlock your car with the app, so you don't have to be present. On average Keyless cars are rented out five times more than non-Keyless cars.

Return the car when you’re done with it

With leasing, you never have to deal with the hassle of buying and selling a car. Once your leasing contract has ended, all you need to do is return the car. Plus if you ever need one again, we're here to help.

How am I insured with GoMore Leasing?

With GoMore Leasing, a comprehensive full insurance is always included in the price. This insurance covers both traffic and liability damages, and includes partial coverage and vehicle damage guarantee. Any deductible is clearly specified in each offer under the "Details" or "Learn more" sections. When leasing your car through GoMore, GoMore's rental insurance is applied, where the renter is responsible for damage costs and deductible in the event of an accident. This insurance ensures a secure and responsible handling of any damages during the rental period.

How does GoMore Keyless work with leasing?

Rent out your leased car without having to be there at pick up and return of the car. With GoMore Keyless, your car is picked up and dropped off through GoMore's app, which makes sharing your car much easier. With GPS tracking and automatic reading of odometer and fuel, you have full control of the car during the rental. Cars with GoMore Keyless are rented out on average 4-5 times more than cars without.

How does the ordering process for a lease car from GoMore work?

Once you have found your perfect leasing car and sent in your inquiry, we will call you to go through any wishes and questions. We will then perform a credit check through UC and send out a digital agreement that you sign with BankID. After you have signed, the relevant partner will contact you with information about the delivery of your leasing car.

How many kilometers are included in the agreement and can I add more?

With GoMore Private Leasing, the specified mileage for each car is specified and when ordering your leasing car you have the option to customise the mileage according to your needs. Any excess kilometers beyond the specified range will be charged upon return of the leasing car. Pricing information can be found clearly under the "Details" section of each leasing offer.

How does service work?

GoMore Leasing always includes service in the price. When your car needs service, you can contact our leasing partner who will refer you to an authorised brand workshop in your area. Service is available throughout the country.

How much can I save on sharing my leasing car?

With GoMore leasing, you can rent out your leasing car when you are not using it yourself. All rentals are insured by GoMore's insurance partner. On average, those who rent out their leasing car through GoMore earn between SEK 2,500-4,500 per month depending on the type of car, where you live and how often the car is available for renter. Write to leasing@gomore.se for an estimate for the particular leasing car you are interested in.
